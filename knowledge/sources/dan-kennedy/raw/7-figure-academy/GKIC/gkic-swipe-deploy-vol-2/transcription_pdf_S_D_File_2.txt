Q: How long does the process take?
A: When a client orders this piece we provide them with a design questionnaire to get them
thinking about how they want to utilize this piece, any design aspects they want to include (logos,
tag lines, etc), get color schemes, etc. Once we get this back from the client, we review and either
start the design process or contact the client with any further questions. We get into the proofing
process and consult with the client until the piece is exactly the way they want it. Depending on the
complexity of the piece this can be as short as 1 proof or potentially several proofs. We find most
clients don't know exactly what they want until they see something completed. Then they are able
to convey their ideas. We try very hard to help the client through the design process and the
questionnaire really helps make this a smooth process. Upon signoff of the artwork we then setup
all the personalization (if any) and print out the piece.
Q: How should we implement this concept into our marketing efforts?
A: It depends on the business and what they are trying to accomplish. This piece is meant to boost
response and retention rates and generate additional revenue from current clients.
Q: What is the approximate cost to get the piece to the customer?
A: It depends on quantity. Pricing starts at $299 plus shipping & handling (see accompanying fax
back from for more pricing info)
Q: What is your minimum quantity to order?
A: 250
Q: Do you have templates I can use?
A: Definitely! Contact us for sample templates.
Q: What sets these apart from other postcards?
A: We have found that our impactful/creative design does make for a greater response rate and the
size of the piece makes a big difference. Bigger is better because it stands out in the mail box. We
advise against sending out a postcard smaller than 6x11. Our customers have reported a 1-2%
increase in response using oversize postcards over the life of the campaign.
Q: Why types of businesses have you worked with that have used Perforated Involvement
Postcards?
A: Chiropractic, Real estate, Retail, Merchant services, Printers , Advertising & Marketing
Agencies, Concert venues, Video production companies, Insurance, Dentists... just to name a few.
