Traffic Arbitrage Supplement:
Keyword Research
About Keyword Research:
System Description: Having a solid keyword list is the lifeblood
of any pay-per-click marketing campaign. This checklist was
designed to assist in the keyword building process. This supple-
ment will cover everything from compiling a keyword list based
on specific niche markets to organizing the keywords into in-
dustry segments. There are several free online tools available to
quickly build substantial keyword lists.
Required Skills: Necessary skills required to perform keyword
list building include basic computer and internet skills, ability
to follow instructions, can organization and sort information in
spreadsheets, segments groups of keywords based on niche mar-
kets, etc.
System Checklist:
Step 1: Build a Keyword Foundation, Video 1
1. Open your internet browser and go to: https://
adwords.google.com/select/KeywordToolExternal
2. Open a new Excel document and create four tabs:
core keywords, search keywords, content keywords
and placement keywords.
3. Determine your cost per click limit – make col-
umn headlines.
