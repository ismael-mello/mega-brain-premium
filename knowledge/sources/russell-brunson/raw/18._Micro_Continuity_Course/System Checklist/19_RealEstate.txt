About Real Estate:
System Description: Our goal is to find REO properties that
will give us the largest refinance profit, sustain the biggest cash
flow, and have the quickest turnaround period from closing to
closing.
Required Skills: The skills that are necessary to be successful us-
ing this program include a willingness to converse with contacts
using phone, fax, and email, the ability to judge whether a deal is
a “good buy,” or a “goodbye” and a dedication to work hard and
never give up on anything you have started.
