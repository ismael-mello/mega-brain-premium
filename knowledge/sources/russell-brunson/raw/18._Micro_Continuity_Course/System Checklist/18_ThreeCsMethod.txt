Recycled Traffic:
Three C’s Method
About the Three C’s Method:
System Description: This system is how you recycle the traffic
that is already on your own list for maximum profits. It is usu-
ally run by the entrepreneur but could be run by someone else.
Required Skills: The person running this system must know
how to write powerful, short and compelling emails that will get
a reader to click on a link to go to the blog. They must also be
able to write and post good pre-selling content to their blog.
System Checklist:
Step 1: Write a Short E-Mail, Video 2
1. Write short, two to three paragraph e-mails with at
least two links pointing back to a blog post.
2. Include a strong call to action and ask them to par-
ticipate in the next step (on the blog).
Step 2: Create a Pre-Selling Blog Post, Video 3
1. This post is where they will be directed from the e-mail.
2. Write it in a way that will get readers to comment.
3. Blog post gives useful but incomplete information.
4. Drives them to a product or service.
5. Use main keywords in the blog title and once or
twice throughout the blog post.
Weekly Action Plan:
How often you write e-mails and
blog posts depends on how much
you want to communicate with
your list. Create your own weekly
action plan and be consistent.
