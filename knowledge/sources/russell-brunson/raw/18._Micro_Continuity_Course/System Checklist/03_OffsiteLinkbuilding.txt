About Offsite Linkbuilding:
System Description: This is a comprehensive offsite search en-
gine optimization system that’s used to accumulate backlinks
from different sources to boost site authority.
Required Skills: No specific skillset is required, although experi-
ence with linkbuilding and the ability to write well is preferred.
System Checklist:
Step 1: Search for Related Blogs, Video 1
1. Go to Technorati.com and search for blogs. Create a
list of the high-traffic blogs you find.
2. Go to Google Blog Search and create a list of blogs.
3. Perform a simple blog search in Google to build a
larger list.
4. Post meaningful comments to the blogs you find
with a link back to your site.
Step 2: Search for Related Forums
1. Go to Google and create a list of forums in your niche.
2. Create meaningful comments in the forums you
find with links back to your site.
Step 3: Directory Submission, Video 2
