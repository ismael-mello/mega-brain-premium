About Press Releases:
System Description: This system will lead you or your employee
through creating a basic press release, submitting it to PRWeb.
com and checking your stats.
Required Skills: It is helpful for the employee to have experi-
ence with writing press releases. However, the ability to write
well is sufficient if the employee follows the steps in this sys-
tem to the letter.
System Checklist:
Step 1: Overview of a Press Release
1. Is the subject of your press release newsworthy
(would news outlets want to pick it up?)
Step 2: Find a Newsworthy Story to Tell
1. Does your press release tie into current events or a
recent news story?
Step 3: Enter Keywords Into Your Press Release
1. Do you have a target keyword for your press release?
2. Do you have two to three secondary keywords?

Step 4: Write Your Headline and Lead
1. Does your press release have an attention-grabbing
headline?
2. Did you put your main keyword at the beginning of
your headline?
3. Does your headline tie your product-service into
your current event?
4. Does the first paragraph of your press release sum-
marize the content of the release?
Step 5: Finish Writing Your Press Release
1. Does your press release have a quote?
2. Does your press release have a bio paragraph?
3. Does your press release have a strong call-to-action?
4. Does your call-to-action tie in with the current event?
5. Is it short and sweet (about 400-500 words)?
Step 6: Submit Your Press Release to PRWeb.com
1. Did you at least use the “$80 submission option”
when submitting to PRweb?
2. Did you submit your release on a Tuesday or a
Wednesday?
3. Did you create a special tracking link so you can
track your release?
4. Did you track conversions from the traffic stemming
from that press release?
Step 7: Checking Your Results
1. Did you track conversions from the traffic stemming
from that press release?
