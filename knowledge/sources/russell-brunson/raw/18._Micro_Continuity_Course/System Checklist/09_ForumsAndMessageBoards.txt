About Forums and Message Boards:
System Description: There are four main goals in forum mar-
keting: start driving traffic through your signature file, create
one-way back links through your signature file, find banner and
placement targeting opportunities and find potential JV part-
ners and affiliates.
Required Skills: The person must be able to communicate well
and know something about the subject. They will be posting
questions and responding to other questions. The goal is to brand
yourself within the community as an expert so that people will
follow your signature file to your squeeze pages.
