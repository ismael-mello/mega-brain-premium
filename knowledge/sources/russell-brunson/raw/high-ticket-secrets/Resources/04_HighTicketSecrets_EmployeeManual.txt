New Hire Probationary Period
As you know, the sales industry is full of exciting opportunities.  However, sales
also require the right kind of attitude, effort, and can present some difficult
challenges. It is because of these challenges that selling is not for everyone.
To ensure success in this industry, new sales employees are required to reach
certain sales goals in order to continue their employment with [Company Name].
During the hire’s probationary period, they will initially be placed on a training
team for the first two weeks of employment.  After the first two weeks the new hire
will be placed on an existing team he or she must:
Put $5000 in sales on the board within 4 weeks.
If this is not accomplished, he or she must:
Put $7500 in sales on the board within 6 weeks.
If goals are not met, it is apparent the relationship between the
new hire and our company will not work out, and we must part
as friends in order to avoid further complication. At any time
during the probationary period new hire may be let go without
reason or notice.
