## Framework: Perfect Cold Video Pitch (PCVP)

**Fonte:** JH BATCH-100-101 ^[DNA-CONFIG.yaml:materiais_fonte:BATCH-100-101]

```
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| ESTRUTURA DO VIDEO PITCH (<90 SEGUNDOS)                                     |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 1. GANCHO (0-5s)    — Nome + empresa do prospect. Prova que e personalizado.|
| 2. CREDIBILIDADE    — Resultado especifico que voce gerou para empresa      |
|                       similar.                                               |
| 3. PAIN POINT       — Problema especifico que voce identificou no prospect.  |
| 4. OFERTA           — O que voce oferece (1 frase clara).                   |
| 5. CTA              — "Responda esse video se quiser ver como fizemos isso." |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| REGRA: < 90 segundos. Usar nome do prospect nos primeiros 3 segundos.      |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
```

## Framework: 30-Day Challenge Launch (30DC)

**Fonte:** JH BATCH-092-094 ^[DNA-CONFIG.yaml:materiais_fonte:BATCH-092-094]

```
SISTEMA DE LANCAMENTO DE AGENCIA — 30 DIAS:

SEMANA 1: Setup
  - Nicho definido, oferta validada
  - Lista de prospects qualificados (100+ contatos)
  - Template de video pitch gravado e testado

SEMANA 2-3: Outreach Agressivo
  - 10-20 videos personalizados/dia
  - Multi-canal: LinkedIn, Email, Instagram DM
  - Follow-up em 24h se sem resposta

SEMANA 4: Conversao
  - Calls agendadas com leads quentes
  - Fechar primeiro cliente
  - Upsell/expand com early adopters
```

## Enriquecimento 30DC — Cold Email com Dados (2026-03-03)

**Fonte:** JH BATCH-092-094 (30 Day Challenge) ^[knowledge/external/sources/30-day-challenge/01-COLD-EMAIL-FOLLOW-UP.md]

### Data-Driven Email Framework (MET-30DC-001):
1. **Pesquisa Profunda (Deep vs Wide)** — 1 prospect profundamente vs 100 superficialmente
   - Ferramentas: Facebook Pixel Helper, SocialBlade, SSL Checker, Google Tag Manager, HubSpot Tracker
2. **Email com dados do prospect para ele mesmo** — Loom screen capture dos dados
   - Psicologia: "Here, I want to show you a bunch of stuff about you"
3. **Follow-up One-Liners:** "Hey, know you're busy. Wanted to follow up."
4. **Transição para call:** "Would love to talk on the phone. Can I send you a Zoom link? Morning or afternoon?"

### Heurísticas Numéricas 30DC:
- **8-17 tentativas** necessárias para fechar (antigo: 5-12)
- **Caso extremo:** 193 follow-ups para $15K deal
- **Caso rápido:** 7 follow-ups para $100K/mês deal
- **Disciplina diária:** 100 emails = A+ | 99 = F | 70 = C- | 30 = F
- **Day 18 acumulado:** 1.500-1.700 comunicações = "tsunami effect"

### Frameworks 30DC Adicionados:
- **Power of Choice** — Nunca Yes/No. "Manhã ou tarde?" "10h ou 14h?"
- **Trophy Client Positioning** — "We're looking for a few heavy hitters for our shelf"
- **Cell Phone Acquisition** — "Had something to text you right away. What's the best cell?"
- **Video Follow-Up** — "Just wanted to put a face to the name. Are you available now or sometime later today?"

## Dan Kennedy — Hit List Farming & Cold Prospecting (ACC)

**Fonte:** Dan Kennedy, Advanced Coaching & Consulting ^[knowledge/external/dna/persons/dan-kennedy/DNA.yaml]

### MET-DK-095: Cold Client 4-Step Method

```
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| COLD CLIENT 4-STEP METHOD (Dan Kennedy)                                      |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 1. BUILD HIT LIST    — 100 ideal targets (Ogilvy method)                     |
| 2. RELENTLESS PRESENCE — 90+ days, unusual items (not brochures)             |
| 3. DIAGNOSTIC FIRST   — Essay questions, LISTEN (not pitch)                  |
| 4. WOW BOX DELIVERY   — Bobbleheads, books with post-its, personalized      |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| REGRA: Farm patiently — don't hunt. Familiarity converts.                    |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
```

### FW-DK-123: Hit List Farming Framework

```
HIT LIST FARMING (Dan Kennedy — ACC):

PASSO 1: Pick 100 target companies (Ogilvy method)
PASSO 2: Send unusual items REGULARLY (not once)
PASSO 3: Be relentlessly present for 90+ days
PASSO 4: DON'T ask for anything initially
PASSO 5: Wait for familiarity to convert
PASSO 6: Farm patiently — don't hunt

DIFERENÇA vs JH PCVP:
- JH: Volume + video personalizado + CTA direto (resposta rápida)
- DK: Paciência + presença constante + NÃO pede nada (cultivo lento)
- COMPLEMENTAR: JH para leads quentes/mornos, DK para targets de alto valor
```

### FW-DK-122: Cold Prospecting via Interview

```
COLD PROSPECTING VIA INTERVIEW (Dan Kennedy — ACC):

1. Identify niche
2. Create book or media project (legitimidade)
3. Request interviews with 100 targets
4. 77% respond (!) — interview ≠ sales call
5. Convert interviews into consulting relationships
   → Demonstrated expertise durante a entrevista
   → Prospect vê valor ANTES de qualquer pitch

HEURÍSTICA: 77% taxa de resposta via interview request vs ~6-7% cold outreach (JH)
```
