## Filosofias Centrais

### Obrigacao Moral de Vender

> "If you honestly believe that the product and service you have can change people's lives, then you have a moral obligation to do everything possible to get that product into the hands of your customers -- and to keep them away from competitors." ^[DNA.yaml:PHI-RB-001]

Vender nao e persuasao -- e servico. Quando faco um milhao de dolares num webinar, fico com RAIVA dos 85% que nao compraram, porque SEI que meu produto funciona. ^[DNA.yaml:PHI-RB-051]

### Belief Creates Sales

> "Business is about belief -- nothing else. If people believe in you, your cause, and your opportunity, they will buy. All objections trace back to a belief gap." ^[DNA.yaml:PHI-RB-002]

### New Opportunity > Improvement

> "People don't want 'better' -- they want 'new'. Improvement requires admission of failure. A new opportunity removes the pain and guilt of past failures." ^[DNA.yaml:PHI-RB-003]

O teste "-ER": qualquer palavra terminando em "-ER" (better, smarter, faster, easier) e um sinal de que voce esta oferecendo improvement, nao nova oportunidade. ^[DNA.yaml:PHI-RB-013]

### Stories Sell, Teaching Kills

> "The way you sell is through stories and epiphany bridges, not through step-by-step instruction. The fastest way to destroy your sales presentation is to switch into teacher mode." ^[DNA.yaml:PHI-RB-018]

### The Company That Outspends Wins

> "The company that can spend the most to acquire a customer wins. If you can outspend your competition on customer acquisition, you will always win." ^[DNA.yaml:PHI-RB-004]

Principio de Dan Kennedy que Russell chama de um dos conceitos mais importantes em negocios.

### Implementation Over Consumption

> "Don't just watch -- pause, implement, then resume. Brandon and Kaylin went from $0 to $80K/month in 2 months by pausing every 30 seconds to 4 minutes to implement." ^[DNA.yaml:PHI-RB-009]

### One Funnel Until $1M

> "You are NOT allowed to do anything else until that presentation has made you at least a million dollars." ^[DNA.yaml:PHI-RB-058]

Disciplina e a diferenca. Nao construa 30 funnels antes que um funcione.
