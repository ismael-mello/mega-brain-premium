### L1 PHILOSOPHIES (estimado ~8-10 novos)
- Change your bait, change your customer — bait atrai dream customer E repele wrong ones
- Business is relationship-based, not transactional — after someone buys, it's beginning not end
- Offer > Product — product is commodity, offer decommoditizes you (Claude Hopkins scheme man)
- People spend more money for exact same content packaged differently (book $10 vs CD $97 vs event $thousands)
- Price elasticity via value increase, not price decrease — Danny Kennedy: no strategic advantage being 2nd lowest price
- One funnel focus — master ONE funnel to $1M before building second
- Every funnel type works for every business — shift question from "this won't work" to "how can I make this work?"
- Perfect Webinar script is THE presentation for ALL funnel types (VSL, webinar, product launch)
- Direct response heritage — all modern funnels descend from direct mail sales letters

### L2 MENTAL MODELS (estimado ~8-10 novos)
- Secret Formula: Who → Where → Bait → Result (4 steps)
- Hook Story Offer as universal diagnostic — if funnel not working, always one of these 3
- Value Ladder: Lead Funnels → Unboxing Funnels → Presentation Funnels → Phone Funnels
- Dream Customer Avatar (Mike & Julie) — find face on Google Images, put on wall
- Congregation theory — dream customers already gathering somewhere (themat.com wrestling analogy)
- Sideways Sales Letter (Jeff Walker) — product launch = sales letter tipped on its side
- Offer as dating analogy — increase offer value, not just "go on date with me"
- Unboxing concept — lay out offer pieces sequentially across funnel steps

### L3 HEURISTICS (estimado ~10-12 novos)
- Lead/Squeeze funnel: 2 pages, generated 250K+ leads/year, built in <10 minutes
- Survey funnel: use when product serves multiple segments that need different messaging
- Summit funnel: upsell = all-access pass, covers ad costs, leads are free
- Book funnel: 2-step order form at top for low price (<$100), below for higher
- Cart funnel: upsell = more of same at discount (not next logical thing like info products)
- Challenge funnel: 30 days, indoctrinates cold traffic into dream customers
- Webinar funnel: #1 recommended first funnel for info/coaching businesses
- Product launch funnel: 4 videos over days, each = section of sales letter
- Phone funnel: for $10K+ products, application-based
- Application funnel: revenue qualifier question to filter prospects
- First ever 2CCX funnel was selling interviews of Vince James ($100M direct mail guy)
- Claude Hopkins paid $52K/year in 1907 ($1.39M equivalent) just to create offers
- 108 Split Tests book: attracted dream customers, repelled wrong ones

### L4 FRAMEWORKS (estimado ~6-8 novos)
- Funnelology Value Ladder Framework: 4 tiers with specific funnel types per tier
  - Tier 1 (Lead): Squeeze, Survey, Summit
  - Tier 2 (Unboxing): Book, Cart, Challenge
  - Tier 3 (Presentation): VSL, Webinar, Product Launch
  - Tier 4 (Phone): Application, Phone Sales
- 9 Ways to Create Info Products: Written (crowdsource, compile examples, public domain) + Audio (audiobooks, interviews, compile hard-to-find) + Video (screen capture, iPhone, workshops)
- Stack Slide Framework: every sale needs a stack slide, even emails
- Offer Creation Bat Meeting: send bat signal, brainstorm all potential products, then select
- Two-Step Order Form: page 1 = shipping info, page 2 = payment (increases conversions)
- Webinar Registration: more curiosity in headline = higher registration rate

### L5 METHODOLOGIES (estimado ~4-6 novos)
- Dream Customer Avatar Method: name avatar, type traits in Google Images, find face, print, put on wall, make all decisions through their lens
- Challenge Funnel Launch Method: daily coaching calls for 30 days, box in mail, video from host + accountability partner
- Affiliate Offer Stacking: don't sell the product (everyone does), create your OWN offer around it
- Summit Funnel Method: interview speakers → registration page → all-access upsell → covers ad costs
